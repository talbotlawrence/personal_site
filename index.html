I would love to meet other I.T. nerds in the Middle Tennessee area.

I currently live in Franklin, Tennessee.

In 2006, I had the privilege of working for the Universal Music Group. I could not have asked for a better job or education. Everyday, I sat next to two help desk administrators, two C# developers, and a database administrator. I have been engrossed in programming and terminal commands ever since.

I decided that I needed to familiarize myself with the local nerds in the middle Tennessee area. The best way to do this is to join technological meetup groups. I first joined the Ruby on Rails meetup group a few years ago and was exposed to Web Development and Test Driven Development. I then joined the Security meetup group and was introduced to Linux and Python.

My goal is to continue to work in an environment that will keep me near cutting-edge technology.
